# PRD: CareTrack Partner Page — Reptile Breeders, Shops, Creators, and Events

## 1. Objective

Create a dedicated **Partner** page for the CareTrack website.

This must not be implemented as a standalone campaign landing page. It should be a normal website page under the CareTrack site structure, accessible from navigation and footer.

The page should explain how CareTrack collaborates with:

- Reptile breeders
- Reptile shops
- Exotic pet / reptile-focused businesses
- Reptile expos and events
- Reptile creators, educators, and influencers

CareTrack also serves normal B2C reptile keepers, but this page should mainly speak to partners who can introduce CareTrack to many reptile owners, buyers, or followers.

The page should position CareTrack as a reptile care continuity and breeding record platform that helps partners support better care after purchase.

---

## 2. Page Type

This page is a **Partner page**, not a landing page.

Do not design it like a one-off advertising landing page.

It should feel like a permanent section of the CareTrack website.

The purpose is to:

- Explain partnership opportunities
- Build trust with breeders, shops, creators, and events
- Highlight CareTrack’s breeding and handover features
- Provide a clear partner inquiry form
- Support SEO for partner-related search intent
- Create a professional page that can be shared during outreach

Is CareTrack only for geckos?

Can breeders use CareTrack for breeding records?

How can reptile shops collaborate with CareTrack?

Reptile shops can collaborate through QR handover, co-branded care guides, customer onboarding support, referral links, or shop display cards that help buyers continue proper care after purchase.

What is a breeder report?

A breeder report is a structured care handover record that may include reptile profile details, feeding history, shed records, weight logs, morph and trait information, breeding notes, and care guidance for the new owner.
FAQ 5

Question:

Can reptile creators collaborate with CareTrack?

Answer:

Yes. Reptile creators and educators can collaborate with CareTrack through educational content, care routine demonstrations, breeding workflow demos, referral campaigns, or responsible reptile care campaigns.

Question:

Does CareTrack replace veterinary advice?

Answer:

No. CareTrack helps organize care records, reminders, and breeding information. It does not diagnose health problems or replace advice from a qualified reptile veterinarian.
FAQ 7

Question:

Can buyers continue records after receiving a reptile?

Answer:

The intended breeder handover workflow allows buyers to continue care records in CareTrack after receiving a reptile, helping them avoid starting from zero.
FAQ 8

Question:

Can CareTrack support reptile expos or events?

Answer:

Yes. CareTrack can support reptile events through QR campaigns, demos, educational content, breeder handover examples, and event-specific collaboration models.
